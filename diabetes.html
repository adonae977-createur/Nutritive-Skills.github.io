Manage Diabetes Through Nutrition

Our specialized programs help individuals with diabetes maintain healthy blood sugar levels through balanced nutrition and lifestyle choices.

Beginner Diabetes Nutrition Program

Start your journey with foundational knowledge about carb counting, glycemic index, and meal planning for diabetes management.

Sample Daily Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and a handful of nuts (45g carbs)
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and olive oil dressing (30g carbs)
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with cucumber slices (15g carbs)
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli (40g carbs)

Total carbs: ~130g (adjust based on your needs)

Advanced Carb Management

Master advanced techniques for precise carb counting, insulin adjustment, and handling special occasions.

Advanced Management Tips

  • Use the glycemic load instead of just glycemic index for better accuracy
  • Combine carbs with protein and fat to slow glucose absorption
  • Monitor blood sugar 2 hours after meals to understand your response
  • Adjust insulin based on planned carb intake, not just what you ate

Weight Management for Diabetics

Learn sustainable weight loss strategies that work with diabetes management and don't spike blood sugar.

Weight Management Strategies

  • Focus on high-volume, low-calorie foods like leafy greens and vegetables
  • Incorporate regular physical activity that you enjoy
  • Practice mindful eating - eat slowly and stop when satisfied, not full
  • Track progress weekly, not daily, to account for natural fluctuations
